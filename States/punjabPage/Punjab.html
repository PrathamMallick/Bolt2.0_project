Punjab

Durries

Flat-woven rugs from India and Pakistan that are often used as carpets on the floor or bed sheets. They are known for their intricate designs, which are inspired by the state of origin, such as multicolor stripes.
Punjabi durries are colorful, attractive, and elegant, and are made using simple horizontal looms and a weft-faced plain weave that gives them a sturdy, flat appearance.

Punjabi Jutti

They are customised initially with leather, rich embroidery made in gold and silver thread. Skilled artisans from remote areas specialise in the making of juttis.
The traditional craft of making Punjabi juttis (footwear) has faced challenges due to changing fashion trends and mass-produced alternatives. Efforts are being made to sustain this craft.
